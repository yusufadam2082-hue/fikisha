Main accounting formulas (backend, source of truth)

1) Customer Total
Customer Total = Item Subtotal + Delivery Fee + Tax Amount + Service Fee + Other Fees

2) Merchant Net Payout
Merchant Net Payout = max(0, Item Subtotal - Platform Fee - Discount Amount + Merchant Tax Adjustment)

3) Driver Payout
Driver Payout = Explicit Driver Payout (if provided), otherwise Delivery Fee

4) Platform Delivery Margin
Platform Delivery Margin = Delivery Fee - Driver Payout

5) Platform Revenue
Platform Revenue = Merchant Platform Fee + Platform Delivery Margin + Service Fee + Other Fees

6) Derived Item Subtotal 
Derived Item Subtotal = max(0, Customer Total - Delivery Fee - Tax Amount + Discount Amount)

7) Derived Merchant Net (fallback when explicit net is missing)
Derived Merchant Net = max(0, Item Subtotal - Platform Fee - Discount Amount + Merchant Tax Adjustment)

8) Payout Eligibility Rule
Payout Eligible = (Order Status is DELIVERED) and (Order is not refunded)

9) Rounding Rule
Final monetary values are rounded to 2 decimal places before persistence.
