Fikisha App Overview (Simple Version)

Fikisha is a hyperlocal delivery app.
That means it connects people with nearby shops and delivers orders quickly within the same local area.

What the app does

1. Customers order from nearby stores
- People open the app, choose products from local shops, and place orders.

2. Stores receive and prepare orders
- Merchants see incoming orders, confirm them, and get them ready for pickup.

3. Drivers deliver to customers
- Drivers are assigned nearby deliveries and update status from pickup to drop-off.

4. Admins manage the whole system
- Admins oversee users, stores, drivers, payments, support, zones, and reports.

How it works

1. Different apps for different people
- Customer app for buying
- Merchant app for store operations
- Driver workflow for deliveries
- Admin dashboard for control and monitoring

2. One central backend
- All apps send requests to a central system that processes orders and updates everyone in real time.

3. Location-based matching
- Orders are matched to nearby stores and drivers to keep delivery fast and local.

4. Secure access by role
- Each user only sees what they are allowed to use (customer, merchant, driver, admin).

5. Tracking and business control
- The platform tracks orders, payments, and operational activity with logs and reports.

One-line summary

Fikisha is a local commerce and delivery platform that helps customers buy from nearby businesses, merchants sell efficiently, drivers earn through deliveries, and admins run everything securely.
